What's this about?




There are different ways to manage, house, secure, and conserve collections, depending on their media and use, and the museum's own discipline, size, facilities, location, financial and human resources. For example, museums might have diverse types of collections that are categorized by different levels of purpose and use like permanent, archival, and educational research which all may have different care and management needs.
